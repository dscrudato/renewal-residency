Is This For You?

Built for those who refuse to settle.

This IS for you if...

You believe your work is part of something greater.

  • You don't want to separate faith from your career
  • You're hungry to grow — spiritually, personally, professionally
  • You want clarity, direction, and mentorship
  • You're in business, healthcare, education, or beyond
  • You want to pursue excellence and contribute to human flourishing
  • You want to play a meaningful role in church planting

This is NOT for you if...

You see work as simply a paycheck and nothing more.

  • You view your job purely as a means to an end
  • You aren't ready to commit to intentional growth
  • You're looking for a low-engagement program
  • You don't believe your work has purpose in God's story

Renewal Residency requires a high level of engagement. It's built for those who are ready to show up.

Program Structure

What does a
month look like?

1

Monthly Cohort Forum

The whole cohort gathers together. You'll hear from different industry speakers and discuss curated content from books, articles, and podcasts explored in your own time.

2

Peer Small Groups

Gather with 1–2 other residents twice a month outside of the forum to discuss content and wrestle with how it applies to your specific work and context.

3

Leadership Development Guide

Meet monthly with your personal guide — someone in your career industry matched specifically to you. They walk alongside you and invest in your growth directly.

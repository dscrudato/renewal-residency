What is Renewal Residency?

Where mission
meets the
marketplace.

Renewal Residency is a program of Summit Church in Raleigh-Durham, NC. We're combining the best of talent development and global mobility from the corporate world with best-in-class ministry and missions leadership development — forming a 2-year residency unlike anything else.

Graduates leave equipped to live out their calling in the marketplace and help bring about kingdom restoration in the world through their daily work.

"If you believe God has more for your work than it just being a means to an end — this is for you."

Program Structure

What does a
month look like?

01

Monthly Cohort Forum

The whole cohort gathers together. Hear from different industry speakers and discuss curated content from books, articles, and podcasts explored in your own time.

02

Peer Small Groups

Gather with 1–2 other residents twice a month outside the forum to discuss content and wrestle with how it applies to your specific work and context.

03

Leadership Development Guide

Meet monthly with your personal guide — someone in your career industry matched specifically to you. They walk alongside you and invest in your growth directly.

Common Questions

Still have
questions?

If you don't see your question here, reach out to Julie directly at jscrudato@summitchurch.com.

Is this only for people at Summit Church?

No — Renewal Residency is open to anyone in the Raleigh-Durham area who shares our values around faith, work, and mission. You don't need to be a member of Summit Church to apply.

How much does it cost?

The program fee is $5,000 per year for a two-year commitment. This covers your guide relationship, cohort forums, curated experiences, and vision trips. If cost is a barrier, reach out — we want to have that conversation.

Can I do this while working full-time?

Yes — the program is designed for working professionals. The monthly commitment is 5–10 hours and all core activities are scheduled with full-time work in mind. The international and domestic trips will require some PTO.

What industries is this designed for?

All of them. We've had residents from business, healthcare, education, tech, law, finance, and more. Your guide will be matched from your specific industry, so the mentorship is always relevant to your context.

When does the next cohort begin?

The next cohort begins in Fall 2026. Applications close May 31, 2026. After submitting your application, you'll be invited to an interest meeting and a brief interview before a final decision is made.

How many people are in a cohort?

Cohorts are kept intentionally small to maintain a high-quality experience and genuine community. We limit enrollment to ensure every resident gets meaningful attention from their guide and real relationships within the cohort.

What happens after the two years?

You become part of the Renewal alumni network — a growing community of professionals who share your conviction that work and faith belong together. We're building something that lasts well beyond the program itself.
